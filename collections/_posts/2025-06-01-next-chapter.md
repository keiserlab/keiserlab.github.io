The Keiser Lab wraps up eleven years at UCSF. Thank you to every lab member, and to the colleagues and friends across UCSF who made it all possible. It's been an incredible journey, and I'm excited about the next one.

Over the past decade, we built AI and machine learning for problems we cared about, publishing [over 60 papers]({{ site.url }}/publications/) and releasing [open-source tools]({{ site.url }}/resources/) to serve the research community. In the next, I'm exploring questions at the core of AI itself.

See what our remarkable [alumni]({{ site.url }}/people/) are up to. I'm now co-founder at [Sygaldry Technologies](https://sygaldry.com), building quantum-accelerated AI.
